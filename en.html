Why Brightlings

Why Parents Choose Us

We combine the best of Montessori philosophy, modern pedagogy, and a warm, nurturing environment to help every child thrive.

Montessori-Inspired Curriculum

Child-led exploration with structured learning milestones. Every activity is intentionally designed to support cognitive, emotional, and social development.

Small Class Sizes

Maximum 12 children per class. Our low teacher-to-student ratio (1:6) ensures every child receives personal attention and individualized care.

Safe & Secure Campus

24/7 CCTV, biometric entry, trained security personnel, and a fully childproofed environment. Your child's safety is our highest priority.

Bilingual Program

English and Bahasa Indonesia instruction from day one. Research-backed bilingual education gives your child a lifelong cognitive advantage.

Holistic Development

Arts, music, movement, nature, and STEM woven throughout the week. We develop the whole child — intellectually, emotionally, and physically.

Transparent Parent Communication

Daily digital reports, parent app updates, monthly progress meetings. You're always informed and included in your child's learning journey.

FAQ

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know before enrolling your child.

What ages do you accept?+
We welcome children from 1 to 6 years old across our four programs: Nursery (1–2), Playgroup (2–3), Preschool (3–4), and Kindergarten (4–6). Each program is carefully designed for the specific developmental stage of that age group.
What are your school hours?+
Core school hours are Monday–Friday, 7:30 AM – 2:00 PM. We also offer optional Extended Care from 2:00–5:00 PM and After School Activities from 3:00–5:00 PM for older students.
How do I enrol my child?+
Start by submitting an inquiry form on this page. Our admissions team will contact you within 24 hours to schedule a campus visit. After the visit, you'll complete our application form and required documents. Admission decisions are typically made within 5–7 business days.
Do you provide meals?+
Yes. We provide a nutritionist-approved morning snack and full lunch daily. Our menus are rotated monthly, allergy-aware, and prepared fresh on-site. Parents receive the monthly menu in advance.
What is the teacher-to-student ratio?+
Our ratio is 1:6 (one educator to six children) across all programs — well above the national minimum standard. Classes are capped at 12 children with two educators per class, ensuring every child receives individual attention.
How do you communicate with parents?+
We use a dedicated parent app (iOS & Android) for daily updates, photos, and learning reports. Monthly parent-teacher meetings are held for in-depth progress discussions. We also maintain an open-door policy and respond to all messages within same business day.
